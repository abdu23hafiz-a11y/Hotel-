The system

Everything the front desk needs.

Six rooms, one key. Each part of Atrium handles a piece of the day so nothing has to live on a whiteboard.

101

Reservations

See every booking, channel, and rate in one calendar. No more tab-switching between booking sites to find a room.

102

Housekeeping

Room status updates the moment a stay starts, ends, or needs attention. Housekeeping always knows what's next.

103

Rate management

Adjust nightly rates by date, season, or occupancy, and push the change to every connected channel at once.

104

Guest messaging

Send check-in instructions, local tips, and checkout reminders by text, without leaving the front desk.

105

Reporting & night audit

Occupancy, ADR, and RevPAR, calculated automatically every night so the numbers are ready by morning.

106

Channel sync

Bookings from every OTA land in one inbox, with no double-bookings and no manual calendar updates.
